Landscaping & Maintenance Services

A well-designed garden thrives with proper care. That's why Lucky Shrub provides professional landscaping and maintenance services to ensure your outdoor space remains lush and healthy year-round. Our experienced landscapers handle everything from irrigation systems to seasonal plant care, keeping your garden in top condition.

A Nursery for Every Plant Lover

Beyond design and landscaping, Lucky Shrub is also home to a charming plant nursery. Managed by co-founder Maria, our nursery offers a variety of indoor and outdoor plants, gardening tools, and expert advice to help plant lovers cultivate their own green spaces.

A Family Passion Turned Business

Founded by husband-and-wife duo Jason and Maria, Lucky Shrub is built on a shared love for plants and design. With Jason's expertise in garden architecture and Maria's marketing and nursery management skills, the company has flourished into a trusted name in the Tucson gardening community. Their dedication to quality and customer satisfaction ensures that every project is completed with passion and precision.
